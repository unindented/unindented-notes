- URL :: https://brave.com/

Web browser that blocks ads and trackers by default. The company behind it, Brave Software, was co-founded by Brendan Eich, the creator of JavaScript, and former CEO of Mozilla.

- Based on Chromium.
- Blocks ads and trackers by default.
- Allows users to enable optional ads in exchange for small amounts of a crypto token.
